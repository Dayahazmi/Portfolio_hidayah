FINAL YEAR PROJECT

Project Overview

Floods are a major problem in Malaysia, causing widespread damage and loss of life.

There is a need to develop accurate and fast flood prediction models to help prevent and mitigate the effects of flooding.

Physical models are expensive and time-consuming to develop, so machine learning algorithms offer a promising alternative.

In this project, K-Nearest Neighbor (KNN) and Decision Tree algorithms will be used to predict floods based on rainfall data.
